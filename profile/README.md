## 🔍 Overview

<a href="#cryptoTaxCalculator">Crypto Tax Calculator</a> automates the calculation of cryptocurrency tax obligations — importing transaction histories from exchanges, wallets, and blockchains, applying the correct accounting methods, and generating the tax reports and forms that cryptocurrency holders need to meet their tax filing requirements.

The <a href="#cryptoTaxCalculator">comprehensive transaction import</a> connects to major cryptocurrency exchanges via API and imports transaction history automatically — buys, sells, trades, transfers, income events, and all other transaction types from Coinbase, Binance, Kraken, and hundreds of other platforms alongside on-chain wallet activity scanned by public address. The complete import eliminates the manual aggregation of transaction records across multiple platforms that cryptocurrency tax calculation requires. The <a href="#cryptoTaxCalculator">cost basis calculation engine</a> applies tax-accepted accounting methods to every transaction — FIFO, LIFO, HIFO, and specific identification — determining the cost basis of each disposal and calculating the resulting capital gain or loss accurately across the complete transaction history.

The <a href="#cryptoTaxCalculator">DeFi and complex transaction handling</a> correctly classifies the varied transaction types that modern cryptocurrency activity generates — cryptocurrency-to-cryptocurrency trades as taxable disposals, staking rewards as income at fair market value on receipt, liquidity pool interactions with their multi-token complexity, yield farming income, NFT purchases and sales with associated gas costs, and wrapped token conversions. Correct classification of these transaction types is where many cryptocurrency tax tools fail, producing inaccurate tax calculations. The <a href="#cryptoTaxCalculator">tax report generation</a> produces the jurisdiction-specific reports and forms required for filing — capital gains summaries, income summaries, transaction-level detail for audit support, and the specific form formats that tax authorities require.
